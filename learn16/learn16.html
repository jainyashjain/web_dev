vh: Relative to 1% of the height of the viewport

px: Absolute unit, fixed size in pixels

em: Relative to the font-size of the element (2em means 2 times the size of the current font)

%: Relative to the parent element

vmin: Relative to 1% of the viewport's smaller dimension

Resize the browser window (both width and height) to see how the font-size of h1 changes. 1vmin = 1vw or 1vh, whichever is smaller.
